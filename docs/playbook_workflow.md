# Workflow Playbook

The goal of this document is to assist team members in setup of various services and establish a common workflow. 

## Tailscale setup

1. Download and install Tailscale for your operating system from https://tailscale.com/download.

2. Sign in or create an account. You may authenticate your account with your school email (Google) if desired.

3. Reach out to Adriean for the link to join the tailnet.

4. Access Nextcloud by navigating to apps.liger-gondola.ts.net:8080.

5. Login with provided Nextcloud credentials.
